Strawberry Milkshake

The best refresher for the upcoming summer.

Recipe

  • 2 cups strawberry ice cream
  • 1 cup fresh strawberries, stemmed and sliced
  • Â½ cup whole milk
  • 1 tablespoon strawberry syrup
  • 3 leaves basil, sliced
  • 1 strawberry

Instructions

  1. Combine ice cream, sliced strawberries, milk, syrup, and basil leaves in a blender; blend until smooth. Pour into a tall milkshake glass and garnish with the whole strawberry.
